Welcome to Urban Sole - where fashion meets comfort with every step you take. We, a team of 6 international students, started our company 1 month ago. The idea behind Urban Sole is that excellent shoes are about far more than appearances; they're about how they feel. We put a lot of attention into making pieces that are flexible without compromising style, so they are comfortable to wear from day to night.

Urban Sole is a community more than just a brand. We want to motivate, connect with you, and support you as you confidently seek your objectives. We also have an intense dedication to moral behavior and sustainability. Each pair of Urban Sole footwear is carefully made using recyclable materials and eco-friendly production techniques, because the planet shouldn't suffer for your style.
